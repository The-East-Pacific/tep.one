We use **[tep.li](https://tep.li)** as our in-house shortlink service for community resources. It gives us clean, memorable URLs.

Common shortlinks include things like:
- `tep.tl/discord` — invite to the Discord
- `tep.tl/forum` — forum

[Visit tep.tl →](https://tep.li)
